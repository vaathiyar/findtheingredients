# P2 — Future Consideration

## 1. Quantity Tracking
Track "3 eggs" instead of just "eggs". Requires unit normalization (grams vs cups vs "a handful"), recipe scaling, and consumption math. Binary have-it-or-don't covers 80% of use cases.

## 2. Pantry Auto-Depletion / Expiry
Auto-remove or deplete ingredients when a recipe is "cooked." Nudges like "you added fresh cilantro 2 weeks ago, still have it?" Error-prone — user may not have cooked it, made half, or bought more without logging.
